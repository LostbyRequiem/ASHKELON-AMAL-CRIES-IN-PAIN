may 21 2026
the machines continue operating.
different uniforms.
different borders.
same architecture of fear.
some people witness violence through shattered apartment windows.
some witness it through bodycams and parking lot arrests.
most witness it through screens until the screens stop meaning anything at all.
everyone is asked to continue normally.
these recordings were made during that normalization.
one piece stretches itself across three movements nineteen minutes:
The Dead Flag Is Raised High.
The Promise To Kill.
The Gravity Is Punishing.
the other piece arrives closer.
a refusal to watch state violence become administrative routine.
The Peace sign burning in the distance.
neither piece is a solution.
only to preserve emotional evidence before it disappears beneath headlines and timelines and institutional language.
there are sounds people learn to live around:
drones overhead.
chanting in streets.
sirens outside apartment windows.
the silence after notifications stop appearing.
eventually history becomes ambience unless someone keeps screaming through it.
this is an attempt to keep screaming.
Ashkelon Amal Cries In Pain.
LostbyRequiem.
